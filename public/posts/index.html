    Odds Ratio Forest Plot (R)

    Feb 10, 2021

    Odds ratios from logistic models are often present using forest plots. Given the coefficients of logistic models are exponentiated to obtain the odds ratios, odds ratios are NOT symmetric on a linear scale. For example,a change from an odds ratio between 0.4 and 0.5 is the same relative change (25% increase) as that between 2.0 and 2.5. But on the linear scale the difference is 0.1 and 0.5 respectively. An odds ratio between 0-1 indicates a decrease of odds, while an odds ratio greater than 1 indicates an increase in odds. ...
