    Collinearity (SAS)

    Apr 12, 2021
    SAS
    Repeated measurements

    Collinearity Collinearity occurs when two variables are highly correlated with each other. Collinearity may cause inflation in standard errors for the given risk factors. However, this is not an issue for prediction purpose. If the collinearity doesn’t involve the risk factors of interest, it’s not a big issue to include the collinear variables for risk adjustment. To check for collinearity, SAS PROC Reg has a convenient option. But SAS logistic or COX do not have corresponding option to check collinearity issues. ...
